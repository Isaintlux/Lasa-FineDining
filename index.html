Our Philosophy

Good food is not a luxury.
It's a right.

We believe every Filipino deserves a beautiful dining experience. At Lasa, we take classic Filipino dishes — the sinigang, the kare-kare, the adobo — and elevate them with care, presentation, and a dining room worth dressing up for. All without the markup that makes fine dining feel out of reach.

Terms & Conditions

Lasa Filipino Kitchen · Effective January 2026

1. Reservations

Reservations are accepted for parties of all sizes. We hold reserved tables for up to 15 minutes past the scheduled time. After this period, we reserve the right to release the table. Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance by calling or messaging us directly.

2. Dining Policy

Outside food and beverages are not permitted on the premises. Lasa reserves the right to refuse service to guests who are disruptive or disrespectful to staff or other diners. We maintain a family-friendly environment at all times.

3. Pricing & Payment

All prices listed are in Philippine Peso (₱) and are inclusive of VAT. Menu prices are subject to change without prior notice. We accept cash and major e-wallets (GCash, Maya). Split billing is available upon request.

4. Allergens & Dietary Needs

Our kitchen handles common allergens including shellfish, peanuts, soy, and gluten. While we take care to accommodate dietary requests, we cannot guarantee a fully allergen-free environment. Please inform your server of any allergies before ordering.

5. Photography & Social Media

Guests are welcome to photograph food and share on social media. We kindly ask that you be considerate of other diners when taking photos. Lasa may occasionally photograph the dining area for promotional purposes; guests who prefer not to be included may inform staff.

6. Contact

For concerns, feedback, or special arrangements, reach us at 0917 888 5272 or visit us at Maasin City, Southern Leyte. We are open daily from 11:00 AM to 10:00 PM.
